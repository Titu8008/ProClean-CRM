The Platform: Next-Generation Service Booking & CRM

Designed specifically for service-based professionals, our platform is a complete operational command center. We seamlessly connect automated online booking and instant quoting directly to a secure, centralized CRM—empowering you to stop chasing leads, close the gap between quote and payment, and run a more profitable business.

Approach A: The "Iframe Widget"

The booking engine can be hosted as a standalone portal or injected into any existing website (Wix, WordPress, Squarespace) via a secure, drop-in Iframe.

  • Data Sync: Automatically retrieves precise property data (like square footage) as customers type their address.
  • Instant Quoting: Calculates exact cost and estimated duration in real-time.

Approach B: The "Hosted Portal"

A client-facing engine designed to maximize conversion rates through a frictionless, transparent checkout process.

  • Dynamic Discounts: Intelligently applies and displays multi-service discounts dynamically.
  • Stripe Checkout: Highly secure, PCI-compliant payment gateway built directly into the flow.

Administrative Command Center

  • Centralized Control: Manage operations, customize rules, and track business growth.
  • Key Metrics: Real-time data including active jobs, MTD revenue, and customer satisfaction scores.
  • Visual Analytics: Easy-to-read charts tracking weekly revenue fluctuations and service distribution.
  • Intuitive Navigation: Quick access to Jobs, Leads & Traffic, Team Members, Scheduling, and Settings.

Job Operations Control

  • Centralized Tracking: Monitor active cleaning contracts, track processing actions, and handle transaction balances from a single screen.
  • Comprehensive Details: Displays unique invoice numbers, customer contact info, and current operational state.
  • Actionable Management: Direct control over the job's status to collect payments, put/release holds, or complete scopes.

Service & Rate Factors

  • Dynamic Pricing Control: Provides vendors with total control over the mathematical formulas used to price their jobs.
  • Granular Configuration: Independently configure custom base rates, square-footage multipliers, fixed-fee charges, and time estimates.
  • Streamlined Actions: Quickly add new services, toggle active visibility, and set the customer-facing display order.

Dispatch & Schedule

  • Automated Slot Reservation: Automatically reserves the exact calendar slots required to complete a secured job.
  • Visual Calendar: A comprehensive weekly calendar view that clearly displays color-coded job blocks and tallies daily working hours.
  • Intuitive Drag-and-Drop: Easily reorganize the fleet's workload to reschedule assignments or dispatch specific units.

System Setup & Branding

  • Custom Branding: Businesses can maintain their unique identity by easily managing customer-facing UI, logos, and color palettes.
  • Core Business Definition: Define operational scope by selecting specific industry types (e.g., "Softwash / Roof Cleaning").
  • Operational Toggles: Granular control over features like toggling the automated "Property Data Lookup" on or off.

Advanced Operational Tools

Promo Codes & Marketing

Drive seasonal revenue with custom promotional codes. Easily track campaign success and automatically apply percentage or flat-rate discounts at checkout to incentivize bookings.

Dynamic Location Factors

Intelligently manage your service areas. The system accounts for location-based factors, ensuring your pricing and availability always align perfectly with the job's distance or territory.

Consumable Estimates

Protect your profit margins by accurately predicting material costs. The system automatically calculates consumable estimates (like chemical mixes or water usage) based on the exact scope and square footage of the job.

Role-Based Access Control

Keep your data secure and your team focused. The dashboard dynamically adjusts its menus and visibility based on the user's specific role—giving field technicians exactly what they need while protecting admin-level financials.
